Frequently Asked Questions

Kwaipilot is a 1 trillion parameter MoE model with 32 billion active parameters, specifically designed for agentic intelligence. It excels in coding, tool calling, and complex reasoning while being fully open source under Modified MIT License.
Our MoE architecture uses 384 experts with 8 selected per token, providing 1 trillion parameter capacity while activating only 32 billion parameters per inference. This achieves excellent performance with efficient resource utilization.
Kwaipilot supports vLLM, SGLang, KTransformers, and TensorRT-LLM. We provide comprehensive deployment guides and examples for each engine to ensure optimal performance in your environment.
Kwaipilot achieves 53.7% on LiveCodeBench, 65.8% on SWE-bench Verified, and 76.5% on AceBench, often outperforming many larger proprietary models in coding and reasoning tasks.
Yes! Kwaipilot is released under Modified MIT License, allowing unrestricted commercial use. You can deploy it in production, modify it, and integrate it into your products freely.
Due to its 1 trillion parameter size, Kwaipilot requires substantial GPU memory. Specific requirements depend on your chosen inference engine and quantization settings. Please refer to our deployment guides for detailed specifications.
